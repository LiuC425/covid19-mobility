The projections are made based on [Google COVID-19 Community Mobility Reports ](https://www.google.com/covid19/mobility/). These reports show how visits and length of stay at different places change compared to a baseline. Changes for each day are compared to a baseline value for that day of the week. The baseline is the median value, for the corresponding day of the week, during the 5- week period Jan 3â€“Feb 6, 2020.
